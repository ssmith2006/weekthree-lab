Who We Are

Welcome to Le Shant Rene' Enterprises! We are the parent company to Le Shant Rene' Designs, a passionate design company, and Phat Girlz Sweets & More, a sweets bakery based in the heart of Baton Rouge. Whether you're looking for creative design solutions or indulging in freshly baked, delicious treats, we've got you covered. Our team, including our fearless leader Shantel Smith, is dedicated to bringing your vision to life through stunning designs and mouthwatering sweets that will leave you coming back for more.

Design Services: From branding and logo design to website creation and graphic design, we work closely with you to ensure your ideas are transformed into beautiful, functional designs.

Baked Goods: Indulge in our delightful selection of cakes, cookies, cupcakes, and other delicious sweets made with love and the finest ingredients.
